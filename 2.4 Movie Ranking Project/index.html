The Best Movies According to Nicole

My top 3 movies of all time.

Phantom of the Opera

A disfigured musical genius who lives in the Paris Opera House becomes obsessed with a young soprano. However, love turns into a murderous rage when his young protege falls in love with another.

What We Do in the Shadows

Viago, Vladislav, Deacon, and Petyr are vampires who share a flat. Deacon's servant Jackie leaves her ex-boyfriend Nick inside. Petyr turns him into a vampire and he soon joins them as a flatmate.

Howl's Moving Castle

Jealous of Sophie's closeness to Howl, a wizard, the Witch of Waste transforms her into an old lady. Sophie must find a way to break the spell with the help of Howl's friends, Calcifer and Markl.
